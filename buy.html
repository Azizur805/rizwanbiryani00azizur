Aksar Pooche Jaane Wale Sawaal (FAQ)

Koi setup fee ya hidden charges hain? ▼
Bilkul nahi. Sirf monthly fee hai — ₹1,500 se ₹2,000 tak, plan ke hisaab se. Koi setup fee nahi, koi hidden charges nahi. Aur koi lock-in nahi — kisi bhi mahine band kar sakte ho.
Customer ko app download karna padega? ▼
Nahi! Ye PWA (Progressive Web App) hai. Customer phone pe website kholega toh "Add to Home Screen" ka option aayega. Ek tap mein app icon install ho jaata hai — native app jaisa experience milta hai bina Play Store / App Store ke.
Mera data safe rahega? ▼
Haan, 100%. Sab data Firebase cloud pe securely stored hai — encrypted aur backed up. Aur sab data sirf aapke control mein hai, koi third-party platform access nahi kar sakta.
Plan switch ya upgrade kar sakte hain? ▼
Bilkul! Kisi bhi waqt Starter se Professional ya Enterprise mein upgrade kar sakte ho. Downgrade bhi ho sakta hai next billing cycle se.
Delivery boys ka panel alag se charge hoga? ▼
Nahi. Delivery boy panel har plan mein included hai. Unlimited riders add kar sakte ho — koi extra charge nahi.
Kitne din mein platform live ho jaayega? ▼
Plan confirm hone ke baad aapka menu, logo, aur details leke platform customize kiya jaayega aur live kar diya jaayega. Developer se WhatsApp pe directly coordinate hota hai.
Payment methods kya hain? ▼
Monthly payment UPI, bank transfer, ya cash mein ho sakti hai. Jo aapko convenient lage.
