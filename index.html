📋 Executive Summary

Rizwan Biryani ek production-ready, full-stack Progressive Web Application (PWA) hai jo restaurant operations ke liye design kiya gaya hai. Ye ek complete digital ecosystem hai jo customer ordering se lekar kitchen operations aur delivery management tak sab handle karta hai.

🚀 रिप्लेस करें: Swiggy/Zomato listing, POS software, Delivery apps, aur Analytics dashboard — Sab ek hi solution mein.
💰 Zero Commission: Ab har order ka 100% paisa seedha aapke account mein.

🏗️ Platform Ke 3 Main Pillar

1. Customer App

  • • PWA: App ki tarah install hota hai.
  • • QR Ordering: Table scan ordering.
  • • Wallet: 5% Automatic Cashback.
  • • Referral: ₹50 bonus for users.
  • • Real-time Tracking: Order status updates.

2. Admin / POS

  • • Dashboard: Revenue aur Profit analytics.
  • • Inventory: Auto-deduct stock system.
  • • KDS: Kitchen Display with sound alerts.
  • • Khata Book: Customer debt tracking.
  • • POS: Offline counter billing.

3. Delivery Panel

  • • Order Accept/Reject system.
  • • Map Tracking via Leaflet.
  • • Rider Earnings Wallet.
  • • Profile & Availability toggle.
